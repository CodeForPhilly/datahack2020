Many people think of recovery by its definition: a return to a normal state of mind, health, or strength. We know this is inaccurate, but many do not. There is no normal. Just as every person is unique, so is their recovery process and definition of it. We chose to follow the story of three personas as they move through the process, allowing viewers to take a more in-depth look at each step by scrolling through the page in order to drive home the point that these are people—just like us.

"The 'success' can look different for everyone! Try not to let your implicit bias come into how you hear these stories. Success might mean that someone goes from using 5 bags of heroin to 3 bags."
